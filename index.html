Professional Profile

José Augusto de Lima Prestes is a senior multidisciplinary executive and researcher with over 25 years of experience at the intersection of technology, governance, digital law, operations, and institutional strategy. On the research front, he currently works both as an independent researcher and as a member of CIENTEC at FEEC/Unicamp, while also providing legal and strategic advisory in innovation-intensive and regulated contexts.

Across the ICT sector, he has held executive and leadership roles including COO, CGO, PM, PO, Head of Legal, and DPO, with direct responsibility for governance architecture, operations, institutional relations, compliance, and large-scale innovation and digital transformation programs.

His practical trajectory includes legal and operational structuring of high-complexity R&D projects, multidisciplinary team leadership, and negotiation with government, academia, and technology companies, as well as management of substantial budgets and STI portfolios.
